# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Internal investors and team operators who need to review, search, share, correct, and follow up on meeting transcripts quickly. They use the product during routine meeting operations and should not need technical knowledge of capture or transcription systems.

## Product Purpose

Capture meetings, turn them into useful transcripts, and make review and follow up dependable. Success means colleagues can understand meeting status, find the right information, and complete common actions with little explanation or manual organization.

## Brand Personality

Calm, direct, trustworthy. The interface should feel like a focused internal tool that handles complexity for the user.

## Anti-references

Dense administration panels, exposed implementation identifiers, decorative SaaS dashboards, repeated explanatory copy, and interfaces that make healthy looking claims when backend work has failed.

## Design Principles

1. Put the common task first and reveal uncommon choices only when needed.
2. Group related actions into one clear workflow.
3. Use plain language and visible outcomes instead of technical metadata.
4. Let automation provide sensible defaults before asking the user to decide.
5. Make failures explicit and actionable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain keyboard access, visible focus states, semantic labels, sufficient contrast, and reduced motion compatibility. Do not rely on color alone to communicate state.
